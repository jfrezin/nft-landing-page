About
XLence and PinUpXL

XLence was born out of PinUpXL, which I started in 2017 before I knew the word NFT. Back then, the original idea of PinUpXL was to create A collection (art series) of fictional advertisements inspired by my love for Pin-Up art, funny ads and curvy women. The original series, which can be found here, was inspired by Spencer David's Booty Babe Art and the voluptuous beauties found in Phil Henderson's art.


This XLence series signifies the resurrection of my PinUpXL brand. We're starting with these bust images featuring only upper-body, but the goal is to create full body images.


Unlike many projects that are using their profits to fund worthy causes, like charity, there's no such plan for this project. In the future, hopefully we'll donate a portion to organizations that help with women's body image issues (I'm very open to suggestions). As for now, the main point of the project is to help me get to a financial position where I can devote myself full-time to working on projects that I'm passionate about, like this one. If you'd like to come with me on this journey, then consider buying an XLence. I have big plans for this series and I can't wait to see them in your hands. Check out the road map. It's different then other projects because it's more long-term than short-term. But I'm excited and I've been thinking of doing these for at least half a decade.
